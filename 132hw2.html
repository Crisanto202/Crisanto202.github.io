SEO tips for beginners

How to make your website appear in google search

  • CRAWLING - This is where google follows links that they already know. Google uses a program called spider. During this process, if your homepage has backlinks from the website that's already registered in google index. The next time google crawls the site, they will follow the link to discover your website and likely add it to their index.
  • MOBILE-FRIENDLYNESS - Web pages must be able to change to mobile. Websites without the mobile version will lead to dissatisfaction.
  • PAGESPEED - How fast your page loads.This will satisfy users and will keep coming back from your website.
  • SREACH INTENT - Follow "3 C's" 1. Content type 2. Content format 3. Content angle. Check ahrefs blog below for more info.
  • BACKLINK - For beginners, it would be best to have informational content. The best way to do that is by having blog links.
  • AUTHORITY - Prioritize strong pages over weak ones. To see this, look at the UR column in the backlinks report. Click on ahrefs blog for more info.
  • CONTENT QUALITY - The use of vocabulary should be understandable for everyone. Most people don't like to read long text and hard vocabulary words. Keep everything simple and readable for audiences.
